gloryah j. allen is a young Black female video installation artist who has and is continuing to push the conversation of racial and gender constraints, juxtapositions, and unities through digital and physical platforms within the state of virginia. she will be presenting her thesis piece, the Black Virginian, on may 5, 2017 in charlottesville, va which highlights the diverse backgrounds and influences of Black individuals.
